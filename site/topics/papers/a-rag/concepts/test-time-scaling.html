    Test-Time Scaling

    TL;DR: A-RAG accuracy improves with max agent steps and reasoning effort — stronger models benefit more from longer horizons.

    Why this matters

    Fixed pipelines cap compute at design time. Agentic interfaces let you spend more test-time compute when the question is hard — the paper's scaling curves validate this on MuSiQue-300.

    Steps vs accuracy

    Drag max steps — watch LLM-Acc climb on the paper's MuSiQue-300 subset (GPT-5-mini).

    Mechanism (§5.1)

    Max steps 5→20: ~+8 pts (GPT-5-mini). Reasoning effort minimal→high: ~+25 pts. GPT-5-mini gains roughly twice as much as GPT-4o-mini from longer horizons — capability and interface compound.
