DCI paper

    Think of it like…

    Finding the right book on the shelf (coverage) vs finding the exact three sentences you need (localization).

    Coverage vs Localization

    TL;DR: DCI often wins by localizing evidence more precisely — not merely surfacing more gold documents.

    Why this matters

    On BrowseComp-Plus, DCI sometimes beats retrieval agents even when both surfaced gold docs — gains come from finer verification steps.

    Coverage vs localization knobs

    Toggle Retriever ↔ DCI. Compare the two bars on the same scenario.

    Mechanism

    Coverage: did the trajectory surface relevant gold documents? Localization: segment-score — how small is the exposed snippet relative to the full gold doc?
